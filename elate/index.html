Program Overview

The Leadership Center for the Sciences and Engineering (LCSE) is a special learning community within Norview High School, geared for gifted, high-achieving students from across the district. LCSE students are enrolled in a four-year rigorous curriculum consisting of honors and Advanced Placement courses, with considerable emphasis in math and science.
